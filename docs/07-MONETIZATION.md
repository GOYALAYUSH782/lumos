# 07 · Monetization & Go-To-Market

> Honest framing: this is a strong **side-project / audience-builder / portfolio
> piece that can throw off cash**, not (on its own) a venture-scale business. Win a
> niche, monetize lightly, build an audience. Reviewed by an indie-founder sub-agent.

## Positioning
Launch with one sharp story: **"Turn your spare monitor into a webcam light — no
ring light, no hardware, free."** Lamp is the searchable utility; **music mode is the
viral hook** (the thing that makes a Reel pop). Launch them together — utility makes
people stay, music makes them share.

## Pricing — freemium, $12 one-time
- **Free forever:** lamp (all warmth/brightness/presets), basic party, music via mic. (Add a small watermark.)
- **Pro — $12 one-time** (NOT subscription; toys churn hard, impulse traffic converts at low one-time prices):
  - No watermark
  - System-audio reactive (vs mic) — validate it works reliably before promising it
  - Multi-monitor **light wall**
  - Premium scene / party packs
  - Software **"movie mode"** bias lighting
- **Don't paywall scheduling** — nobody pays to schedule a light; keep it free.
- Unlock via Lemon Squeezy / Gumroad license key. Capture **email** at the upgrade moment.

## Channels (sequence matters)
1. **Short-form video (TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts) — the engine.** Run continuously
   from day one, not as a one-time beat. The product is instantly visual ("wait, that's free?"). Lead with music mode.
2. **Reddit** — r/InternetIsBeautiful, r/battlestations, r/macsetups, r/WFH. Post as "I made this free thing," never lead with the upsell. High-variance spike.
3. **Product Hunt** — worth ~half a day for the backlink + "as seen on" credibility. A one-day spike, **not** a durable channel. Don't build the launch around it.
4. **Show HN** — one afternoon; technical credibility + backlink.
5. **SEO (compounding, 6–18 mo)** — winnable queries: "monitor as a lamp", "webcam light no ring light", "free zoom lighting", "music reactive lights browser", "second monitor light". Avoid saturated "ring light"/"party lights".
6. **App stores (later, optional)** — wrap as a Mac App Store app once there's traction (also unlocks real system audio).

## Realistic expectations
- Conversion on free aesthetic tools ~0.5–2%. At $12, a sustained 50k visitors/mo
  might yield ~$1–2k/mo — spiky, tied to video wins. Steady state for a solo
  side-project: a few hundred to low-thousands/mo.
- **Reframe success as audience + validation**, not ARR. A tool that gets 100k+ video
  views is a credibility/audience machine; the email list + proven willingness-to-pay
  de-risk the next, bigger thing. Cost to run ≈ $0–15/mo (static hosting + domain).

## First 30 days (suggested)
- Wk 1: name/trademark check + domain; deploy free version to Vercel/Cloudflare (HTTPS).
- Wk 2: landing page, 30-sec demo video, OG image, analytics (Plausible), Pro paywall stub + checkout.
- Wk 3: soft launch on Reddit + X; gather feedback; start video reps.
- Wk 4: Product Hunt + Show HN same week; push video; begin SEO pages.

## Top 3 actions
1. Ship **Lamp + Music together**; lead all marketing with the music demo; run short-form video continuously.
2. Paywall **vanity/hard features at $12 one-time** (no watermark, system-audio, multi-monitor, scene packs); validate system audio first.
3. Treat it as **audience-building**: email capture at upgrade; PH/HN/Reddit as backlink spikes; judge by list growth + viral reps, revenue as bonus.
